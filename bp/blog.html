Synod Wrap Up, Final Numbers, and What's Next

Posted July 7, 2011 12:31 PM by Richard and Maxwell Brooke    

 "There are those who give with joy, and that joy is their reward." Kahlil Gibran

Hello everyone!

As we sit here in our living room of China Grove, NC, reminiscing of our trip with our parents, we are reminded of the many gifts that we were given on this journey. And one of the greatest gifts was our time in Tampa at General Synod. 

We screened our feature again on July 4th in the exhibit hall and then we had two more presentations. The first was for a dinner with Wider Church Ministries. The second was for the morning plenary on July 5th. We spoke about our time traveling and how supporting our churches and communities make a positive impact in our lives. For a more eloquent rendition of what we said, I turn your attention to this article that was written by Jeff Woodard.
