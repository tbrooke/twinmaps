The video for the United Church AIDS/HIV Network (UCAN) and the AIDS Service Center NYC (ASC) is posted!

UCAN's mission is to build a network of people, congregations and organizations within and beyond the UCC for care-giving, education, and prevention in response to the HIV/AIDS virus. 

ASC is a multiservice community organization that carries out its mission of "helping many, one by one" by building community, connection, and stability for New Yorkers living and at-risk for HIV/AIDS.

The Flood Comes...

Posted December 8, 2010 by Richard and Maxwell Brooke

 "Every act of creation is first an act of destruction." Pablo Picasso

Ya know, we've been staring at this screen for the past few hours trying to get the words right for what we want to say about what we've seen. 

And we can't do it. 

We've been on this project since September and have been extraordinarily blessed. We have had our thoughts and values reconstructed, met people that change the lives of others daily, had our breath taken away weekly and have seen things that have moved our very souls. Because of that, we are always in a state of mind where we can share what we've seen in a clear, concise manner but with just enough emotion to know that we are changing with every step of this trip. Every time we think we are going to be sent over the edge in emotion, we find some sobering tonic, whether it be the people, the project, or our own professionalism, to bring us back down. 

But not this time. 

This time, we don't know what to say.
