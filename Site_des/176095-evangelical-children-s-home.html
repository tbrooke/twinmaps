Programs & Services

ECH offers a wide variety of care. Everything from residential treatment to on-grounds schooling to counseling, we offer something for each person we serve, no matter what their age or their need.
 

Our staff is dedicated to providing children with solid and substantial growth experiences which will prepare them for effective personal, family, community and career interactions. We provide caring, professional services that empower, educate and nurture children, youth and families to become productive, healthy members of the community.
 

ECH gives each child and family a chance to make a difference in their lives. A chance to make a fresh start and strive for a better life than the one they have led.
 

We offer the entire family the opportunity of a lifetime, to start over and we believe that every member of the family deserves a chance at happiness and harmony.
