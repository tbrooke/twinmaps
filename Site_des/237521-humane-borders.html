Welcome To Humane Borders

Humane Borders, motivated by faith, offers humanitarian assistance to those in need through the deployment emergency water stations on and near the U.S.-Mexico border. To learn about our history, organization, leaders and activities, click here.

"They will neither hunger nor thirst, nor will the desert heat or the sun beat upon them. He who has compassion on them will guide them and lead them beside springs of water."

         -- Isaiah 49:10

For Volunteers

Hundreds of volunteers bring our mission to life, servicing our water stations, staffing our offices in Tucson, performing maintenance on our equipment and other tasks. To learn how to volunteer or intern with us, click here.

For Supporters

Maintaining life-saving water stations in the borderlands takes financial support. Humane Borders welcomes financial and in-kind donations. To learn how to make a financial contribution, or purchase merchandise that supports our mission, click here.

YouTube, Facebook, Yahoo Groups

Humane Borders is now on Facebook. Friend us to find and share the latest news. You can also keep up with developments by joining Humane Borders News at Yahoo Groups. And, Humane Borders now has videos posted on its HB Volunteer channel on YouTube!
